Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CA Tax Calculator ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our free online tax calculator at this website.

1. Information We Collect

The tax calculator runs entirely in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the financial figures you enter into the calculator. All calculations happen locally on your device.

We may automatically collect limited technical data through third-party services (see below), including:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • Device type (desktop/mobile)

2. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site (e.g., which pages are most popular, how long visitors stay). Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous usage data. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. Google AdSense and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on this website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other sites on the internet. These cookies allow Google and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or www.aboutads.info/choices/.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

Our website itself does not set cookies. Third-party services (Google Analytics, Google AdSense) may set cookies as described above. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent any personal information to third parties. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (name, email, SSN, financial account numbers) through this website.

6. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Because we do not sell personal information and collect only anonymized analytics data through third-party services, most CCPA provisions are satisfied by design. For any privacy inquiries, please contact us at the address below.
